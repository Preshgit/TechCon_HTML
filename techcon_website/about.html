History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into a global conference bringing together the brightest minds in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

TechCon strives to inspire and empower individuals and organizations by showcasing technological advancements and fostering innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech community.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

A pioneer in computing, Ada Lovelace was one of the first to recognize the potential of computers to go beyond numerical calculations.

Alan Turing

Widely regarded as the father of artificial intelligence, Alan Turing made groundbreaking contributions to computer science and cryptography.
